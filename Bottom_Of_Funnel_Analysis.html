AI Recruiter – Bottom of Funnel Analysis

Date Range Analyzed • [2025-04-01 → 2026-01-31]

Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates Bottom-of-Funnel (BOF) performance for both Alex and Sense AI recruiting platforms across the critical conversion stages from approximately April 2025 through January 2026, covering 313 total client submissions.

The combined platform achieved a 17.9% submission-to-interview conversion rate with 56 total interviews secured, demonstrating effective initial qualification and client-candidate matching. Interview quality translated to a strong 19.6% interview-to-offer rate (11 offers extended), indicating well-prepared candidates reaching final stages.

Most notably, the platforms achieved a robust 63.6% offer acceptance rate with 7 successful hires from 11 offers, significantly outperforming typical industry benchmarks of 40-50% and suggesting excellent candidate engagement, competitive positioning, and offer presentation throughout the recruitment journey.

Sense demonstrated superior conversion efficiency with 2.89% end-to-end conversion (submission to hire) compared to Alex's 1.43%, while processing 23% more submissions. This suggests Sense's real-time engagement model may provide advantages in candidate quality and commitment at the bottom of funnel stages.

The exceptionally high offer acceptance rate (63.6%) indicates both platforms excel at candidate relationship management and setting appropriate expectations throughout the process, reducing offer decline risk and maximizing hiring efficiency.
